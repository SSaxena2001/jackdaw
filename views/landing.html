Welcome to the Doujinshi Code

Here at the Doujinshi Code, we sit around and discuss about the latest Doujins that have been released. They may be from any anime or fan-fiction or anything, we do not judge. In fact, we revel in these matters. So enjoy your time here -Kurabu Kaichou Nikiru

Chika Fujiwara

She is the Student Council Secretary and the Tabletop Gaming Club Member at Shuchi'in Academy

Kaguya Shinomiya

She is the Vice President of Student Counci, and a member of the Archery Club at Shuchi'in Academy

Tatsumaki

She is the S-Class Rank 2 professional hero of the Hero Association, an esper, and the older sister of Fubuki.

Tadokoro Megumi

Tadokoro Megumi is a 92nd Tōtsuki Generation student, a Polar Star Dormitory resident, and the 10th seat of the Elite Ten Council at Totsuki Saryo Culinary Institute.

Kyoka Jiro

Kyoka Jiro, also known as the Hearing Hero: Earphone Jack, is a Hero Course student in Class 1A of the U.A. High School.

Uraraka Ochaco

Uraraka Ochaco, also known as Uravity,is a student at U.A. High School training to become a Pro Hero. She is one of Izuku Midoriya's closest friends.

Asuna Yuuki

Asuna Yuuki, more commonly known as just Asuna, her player name in the game Sword Art Online, Asuna appears as the lover of Kirito as well as the female lead. She is the sub-leader of the Knights of the Blood Oath Guild, notable for being the strongest guild in Aincrad.
